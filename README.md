## PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project focuses on analyzing sales, customer data and staff performance across four major regions in Nigeria: North, South, East, and West and presenting key insights and findings.


## OBJECTIVE

* Analyze the total revenue 
* Analyze revenue for each region
* Analyze revenue for each category
*	Identify sales trend across the year
*	Identify the total unit sold 
*	Unit sold for each category
*	Identify the top 5 performing staff 
*	Identify the top 5 customers


## TOOLS USED

-	Functions (Find and Replace)
-	Date correction (manual entry and drilled down
*	Pivot table – For aggregation
*	Slicers – For filtering, responsiveness and easy navigation
