## PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project focuses on analyzing sales, customer data and staff performance across four major regions in Nigeria: North, South, East, and West and presenting key insights and findings.


## OBJECTIVE

* Analyze the total revenue 
* Analyze revenue for each region
* Analyze revenue for each category
*	Identify sales trend across the year
*	Identify the total unit sold 
*	Unit sold for each category
*	Identify the top 5 performing staff 
*	Identify the top 5 customers


## TOOLS USED

*	Excel – For data extraction,
  *  Data profiling and cleaning using;
  * Nesting for standardization (Tables, Proper, Clean, Trim, Substitute)
  * Functions (Find and Replace)
  * Date correction (manual entry and drilled down
*	Pivot table – For aggregation
*	Slicers – For filtering, responsiveness and easy navigation
*	Charts and shapes – For visualization

## KEY INSIGHT

*	Top category by revenue is computer
*	Top category by units sold is Mobile with 308 units
*	Best selling product by unit sold is Tablet T8 with 226 units
*	Top region by revenue is West ($15,54M) while the lowest region is North ($39.49M). This shows strong regional imbalance showing that west is the most lucrative.
*	Peak month is June ($124.83M revenue) and lowest month is April ($2.1M revenue). Revenue spiked dramatically mid-year, indicating possible seasonal demands or major deals.
*	Top Customer is Omega Wholesale ($89.56M) which accounts for nearly 30% of total revenue. Top 5 contribute $203M, which is 67% of total revenue.
*	Best Performing staff is Peter Obi with $87.79M revenue generated.


## RECOMMENDATION

*	North region and Audio category show potential for improvement. Marketing strategy should be set up for the promotion of products in regions with less sales. 
*	June to August are strong months, promotions during this period should be leveraged as it could maximize sales.
